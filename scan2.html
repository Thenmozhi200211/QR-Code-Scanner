A Call-2-Action under the QR code increases the chance that it will be scanne.

QR Codes Need Direction

On their own, QR Codes are just sitting there — passive and unnoticed. It's up to you to spark action by letting people know why they should scan.

Guide Your Users

Add a clear call-to-action that tells them exactly what to do and what happens when they scan. You can place this message around the QR Code or in the center if it's logo-free.

Make It Clear & Engaging

Your call-to-action should be direct and appealing. Use phrases that set clear expectations, such as:

  • “Scan to Connect to Wi-Fi”
  • “Scan to Pay Instantly”
  • “Scan for 10% Off”

This not only informs but also motivates users to take action right away.
